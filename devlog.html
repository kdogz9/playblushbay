Devlog #2: Designing the First Fish Diagnostics Grid

Programming 💻

Today we worked on the isometric user interface for when you treat fish. We designed a cute pop-up medical kit menu! Players can click on their sick clownfish to check their temperature and apply sparkly medicine cream. It looks incredibly sweet, and we can't wait to show off the pixel art animation next month!
