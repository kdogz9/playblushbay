Log #2: Designing the Pastel Shop Items 🛍️

Posted on: May 15, 2026

Today we worked on the item shop assets! We designed three types of isometric pink castles and customizable neon signs that players can place inside their tanks. The visitor AI is now officially donating coins when they stop to look at happy fish!

Log #1: The Journey Begins! 🐟

Posted on: April 20, 2026

We have officially started working on Bubble & Bloom! The core isometric grid system is up and running. We chose our color palette—heavy on the soft pinks, mint greens, and lavender tones. Steam page release is locked in for May next year!
