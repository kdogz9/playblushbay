The Healing of Coral Cove 🌸

Once upon a time, Coral Cove was the brightest gem in the ocean, filled with shimmering pastel reefs and joyful sea creatures. But over time, the colors began to fade, and the fish became sick due to mysterious pollution.


You play as a passionate marine biologist who inherits an abandoned shoreline observatory. Armed with nothing but a love for sea life and an eye for cute interior design, it’s up to you to build a safe haven.


"Every fish returned to the sea carries a spark of magic that heals the reef."

The more fish you care for, the more the magic spreads, revealing legendary hidden species like the Glittering Jellyfish and the Pastel Star-Whale!
