Lore

The bay used to sparkle.

Blush Bay was once full of glowing coral, colourful fish, and crystal-clear water. But slowly, the ocean began to lose its colour. Fish became weak, reefs faded, and the shoreline grew quiet.

You open a tiny rescue aquarium by the sea. With every fish healed and released, the bay begins to bloom again.

The Player’s Role

You are the new aquarium keeper of Blush Bay. Your job is not just to display fish, but to protect them, heal them, and return them home. The aquarium becomes a cosy rescue centre where visitors come to support your mission.
