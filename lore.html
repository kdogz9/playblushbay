The Legends of the Deep 🌊

Step into a world where your love for aquatic life has the power to heal an entire ocean ecosystem. Here is the backstory of the world you will be saving:

The Dull Pollution 🖤

Once upon a time, Coral Cove was a breathtaking marine paradise filled with shimmering pastel reefs. Over time, a mysterious, color-sapping pollution crept across the ocean floor. It stripped away the vibrant pinks and purples, leaving the corals grey and making the local fish sick and weak.

"When the last coral loses its glow, the sea will fall into a deep sleep..."

The Coral Sanctuary 🌸

Perched on the edge of the cove sits an abandoned marine observatory. Equipped with specialized, cozy healing tanks, this laboratory serves as a safe haven. Inside, protected from the rough, polluted waters of the open sea, sick sea creatures can safely recuperate under your watchful eye.

The Magic of Restoration ✨

The connection between the reef and its creatures is magical. When a fish is fully rehabilitated and happily released back into the wild, it carries a spark of life force back to the seabed. With every creature returned, the grey corruption recedes, permanently restoring brilliant ecosystems and calling legendary sea giants back home.
