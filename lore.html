The Story of Blush Bay 🐚

"Once upon a time, Blush Bay was famous for its glowing pink neon reefs and magical sparkling waters. But over time, the colors began to fade..."

The Fading Reef

Blush Bay used to be a bustling, glowing sanctuary protected by a community of happy sea creatures. Due to pollution and neglect from nearby industrial towns, the coral reefs have lost their magical sparkle, turning a sad, dull gray. The fish have become sick, and the bay needs a hero.

Your Mission

You arrive as the bay's new resident marine biologist and decorator. Armed with nothing but a love for nature and a great eye for design, you set out to build a world-class rescue center. By healing the wildlife and reintroducing them to the bay, your actions directly restore the vibrant pastel glow to the ocean environment, patch by patch!
