✿ The Story ✿

🌸 The Ocean's Cry

Once upon a time, the Coral Sea sparkled with life and color. Fish of every hue danced through crystal waters, and the reef sang with joy. But as the years passed, shadows crept into the depths...

Pollution, warming waters, and careless hands have left many sea creatures sick and lost. The ocean's heart grows quieter each day.

💖 Your Calling

You've inherited your grandmother's seaside aquarium—along with her gift for understanding sea creatures. She believed that every fish deserves a second chance, and now that mission is yours.

🌊 A New Hope

One by one, you'll rescue the ocean's forgotten friends. Heal their wounds, learn their stories, and when they're ready, set them free. Each release sends ripples of hope through the sea, slowly bringing the Coral Sea back to life.

"Every creature healed is a star returned to the sea." 🌟

— Grandmother's Journal
