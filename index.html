Storybook & Character Diary

Uncover the magical ocean secrets of the historical Blush Bay!

🔖 🎀

Once Upon a Blush Bay...

Legend holds that the tides of Blush Bay were not always regular blue water. Decades ago, the bay was a glowing, bioluminescent pink ocean haven fed by magical crystalline reefs. Marine species from across the globes migrated here specifically to raise their young inside the warm, sparkling waters.

But as machinery and factories crowded the mainland, soot and plastic sheets sank into the seabed. The glowing pink coral reefs grew dark, and the cozy critters started to fall ill. The legendary light faded.

Now, as the newly appointed sanctuary director, your compassion holds the key to triggering a biological recovery. Every fish you nurture, raise, and happily release carries specialized minerals that slowly dissolve in the tide, restoring Blush Bay back into a shimmering pastel paradise!

Our Vision for Blush Bay

Development has officially bloomed! We wanted to craft an environment that offers absolute tranquility. Instead of placing pressure on players with harsh failing timers or game-overs, we focused heavily on therapeutic interactions—saving injured marine life brings a high sense of rewarding accomplishment, and styling custom tanks lets your imagination wander.
