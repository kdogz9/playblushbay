Game Features

Rescue & Heal

Take in sick, injured, or sad ocean critters. Diagnose their ailments, feed them treat-filled meals, and nurse them back to perfect health!

Restore the Ocean

Once your fish are thriving, release them back into the wild to breathe life, color, and joy back into a fading coral reef ecosystem.
