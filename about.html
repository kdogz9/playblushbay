How to Play 🐠

In Bubble & Bloom, you run a boutique marine sanctuary. The gameplay relies on three cozy pillars:


🩺 Rescue & Rehabilitate

Receive sick, stressed, or injured sea life. Diagnose their ailments, set up the perfect water parameters, and feed them their favorite treats until they are swimming happily again.


🎨 Decorate with Girly Pop Flair

Earn donations from delighted aquarium visitors! Use your funds at the shop to buy pastel corals, cute glowing neon signs, pink castles, and sparkly floor paths to design the ultimate isometric paradise.


🌊 Restore the Ocean

Once your fish are fully healed, release them back into the wild. Watch as the dead grey ocean reefs outside your windows slowly transform back into vibrant, colorful ecosystems.
