About Blush Bay Ocean Rescue 🎀

Blush Bay Ocean Rescue is an isometric pixel art aquarium management simulator designed to be the ultimate cozy gaming experience. It blends low-stress management mechanics with vibrant, girly-pop aesthetics.

As the head caretaker of a run-down marine sanctuary, your job is to turn it into a pastel paradise for marine life and visitors alike!

Core Gameplay Loop:

  • Rescue: Scout the bay for hurt, plastic-tangled, or sick fish.
  • Rehabilitate: Place them in specialized quarantine tanks, administer medicine, and feed them their favorite treats.
  • Customize: Decorate your aquarium with sparkling crystals, pink corals, cute neon lighting, and adorable sunken castles.
  • Release: Watch a heartwarming cutscene as you wave goodbye to your healthy fish friends, sending them back to permanently heal the ocean reef.
