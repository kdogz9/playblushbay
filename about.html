About the Game

A soft aquarium sim about care, colour, and second chances.

Build your aquarium from a tiny rescue room into a magical pastel sea-life sanctuary. Every fish you help brings Blush Bay closer to becoming healthy again.

Rescue Fish

Find sick fish in the ocean and bring them safely into your aquarium.

Care for Them

Place fish in tanks, monitor their health, and help them recover over time.

Decorate

Use visitor donations to buy cute decorations, plants, shells, lights, and cosy tank items.

Core Gameplay Loop
